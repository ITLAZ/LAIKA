We Are Laika

About Laika

Laika is more than just a shelter; it is a community dedicated to the well-being of animals. Founded with the belief that every life is precious, we work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and abandoned pets.

Our Mission

To end animal homelessness and suffering through rescue, rehabilitation, and education.

Our Vision

A society where compassion for animals is a core value and responsible guardianship is the norm.
