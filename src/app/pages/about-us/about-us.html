About Laika

Laika is more than just a shelter; it is a community dedicated to the well-being of animals. Founded with the belief that every life is precious, we work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray and abandoned pets. Our facility provides a safe haven where animals receive medical attention, nourishment, and love until they find their forever families.

Our Mission

To end animal homelessness and suffering through rescue, rehabilitation, and education. We strive to create a world where every companion animal has a safe and loving home.

Our Vision

A society where compassion for animals is a core value, and where shelters are no longer needed because every pet is cherished and responsible guardianship is the norm.
